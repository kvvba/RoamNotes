#+title: Overall efficiency

\eta_O = (power to the aircraft)/(power available from fuel)

\eta_p = \eta_p \cdot \eta_th

See [[id:7ba9790c-aa85-443d-b250-07b85b094ebe][Propulsive efficiency]], [[id:9822388d-f72a-4a55-be87-a1c2f505dee8][Thermal efficiency]].

Can also be written as:

\eta_O = (thrust \times speed)/(m_f' LCV) = (1/SFC) \cdot (V_0)/(LCV)

Where LCV is the lower calorific value of the fuel (see [[id:9822388d-f72a-4a55-be87-a1c2f505dee8][Thermal efficiency]]) and SFC is [[id:da543acc-5803-4881-bb4e-b475f69a38d8][Specific fuel consumption]].
