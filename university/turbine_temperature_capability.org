#+title: Turbine temperature capability

In order of decreasing temperature capability:
1. New material, advanced cooling and thermal barrier coating
2. Single crystal material, advanced cooling and thermal barrier coating (TBC)
3. Single crystal material, advanced cooling
4. Single crystal material, simple cooling
5. Directionally solidified material, simple coating
6. Directionally solidified material, uncooled
