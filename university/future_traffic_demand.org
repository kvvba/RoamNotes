#+title: Future traffic demand

- The continued growth in traffic demand is generally agreed by all sections of the industry
- The major drivers are:
  - Travel for pleasure
    - Growth in GDP
    - Wealthy and fit 60/70 year olds
  - Global trade
    - Emerging nations
    - Distribution of natural resources
    - Low cost centres of manufacture
- Major considerations have been point-to-point or hub-and-spoke
  - Point to point benefits from speed and economies of twin-engine aircraft
