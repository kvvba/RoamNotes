#+title: Accident and serious incident investigation

The causes of an aircraft accident or serious incident must be identified in order to prevent repeated occurrences. The identification of causal factors is best accomplished through a formal investigation. Annex 13 states that the primary objective of the investigation of an [[id:1b40fd94-2b00-46e1-891d-b0e46b44c77c][Aircraft accident]] or [[id:81dcfca6-fe44-47d8-8392-d3ef7cd789ee][Aircraft incident]] is prevention, but can result in prosecution.
