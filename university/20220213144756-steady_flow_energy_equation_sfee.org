#+title: Steady flow energy equation (SFEE)

h_1 + (1/2)v_1^2 = h_2 + (1/2)v_2^2
Where h [J/kg] is specific enthalpy.

h = c_p T
Where c_p [J/kgK] is the specific heat capacity at constant pressure.

Assuming no work or heat transfer into or out of the system.
