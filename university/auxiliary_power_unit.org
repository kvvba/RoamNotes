#+title: Auxiliary power unit

Small gas turbine located in tail of the aircraft.

Provides power for:
- Main engine starting
- Supply of cooling air in hot climates
- Supply of electrical power when main engines are shut down
- Air-conditioning
- Powers electrical systems in case of main engine flame out
