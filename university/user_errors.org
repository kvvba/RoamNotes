#+title: User errors

Incorrect use of CFD and related codes

- Oversimplification of problem
- Poor grid generation
- Incorrect boundary conditions
- Inadequate solver parameters (e.g. timestep)
- Acceptance of non-converged solutions
- Post processing errors

Causes:
- Lack of experience
- Lack of attention to detail or other mistake
- Lack of resources
- Poor project management
