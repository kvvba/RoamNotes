#+title: Incremental innovation

- Most common form of innovation
- Responsive to problems, opportunities or trends
- Maintains or enhances competitive position
- Lower risk
- Similar aircraft architecture and better propulsion systems
- The assured option
- As opposed to [[id:660c7b48-8b5f-42bc-8e83-188817e0a04b][Radical innovation]]
